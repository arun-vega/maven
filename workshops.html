Specialist Talks and presentations

As part of our commitment to increasing the understanding of the public about varicose veins, thread veins, leg ulcers, and other venous disease, including the latest and best ways of treating them, we are happy to provide specialists to give talks or presentations to groups of interested people. If you would like to ask for a specialist to attend a meeting and give a talk or presentation, please contact us on info@mavenmedicalcenter.com with details of the meeting, audience and subject matter in which that you would be most interested.
