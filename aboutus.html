About Us

12 years of experience

Welcome To Maven Medical Center

Maven Medical Centre is a state of an art stand alone daycare surgical facility located in the heart of city in Hyderabad. It is first of its kind to give exclusive services in superspeciality fields of laser treatment for varicose veins, minimal invasive proctology (piles, anal fissure, anal fistula) and daycare laparoscopy surgery for gynaecological & general surgical diseases. The pillars of this center are dedicated team of highly qualified and well experienced interventional radiologists and senior surgical specialists. All the modalities of treatment here are time tested, scientific & evidence based as per the International guidelines. To its credit, this facility is also a recognised training centre in our country for laser treatment where doctors from across the globe participate in the regular workshops to update their skills.
